There is one very special type of personality, called psychopaths (similar to sociopaths).

You will most probably meet one, or maybe you already have. If you have, you will likely not forget it. To meet a psychopath is usually a very traumatic experience.

The fascinating yet terrifying thing with psychopaths is that almost none of us can detect it. Most of us would be very much charmed by them.

Then, suddenly and unexpectedly the charmer and “friend” can turn your life into a real inferno!

The basics of identifying psychopaths are something you and anybody else should know. One of the extracurricular activities in this program is the “psychopath school”.

Of course not to learn how to be one but how to see one ;) 
